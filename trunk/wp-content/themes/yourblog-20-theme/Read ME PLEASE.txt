Installation Instructions

- Unzip the contents of the zip.
- Copy the whole web20 folder into your wordpress's wp-content/themes/ folder
- Go to the Presentation area of your WordPress Admin and Select the 'Web 2.0' theme.
- Goto Write > Pages & Create pages for About, Archives, Links and Contact.
- You are done.

Make Sure you donot rename the web20 folder
